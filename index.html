Leafy Vegetables & Citrus Fruits

Introduction

Leafy vegetables and citrus fruits are important components of a healthy and balanced diet. Leafy vegetables provide essential vitamins, minerals, fibre and antioxidants. Citrus fruits are widely known for their high vitamin C content and refreshing taste which supports immunity and overall health.

Leafy Vegetable Recipes

Creamed Spinach

Ingredients

  • Fresh spinach
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Cream
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  • Wash spinach thoroughly
  • Cook spinach briefly until wilted.
  • Melt butter in a pan and sauté garlic
  • Add spinach and mix well
  • Add cream and cook until slightly thickened
  • Season and serve

Braised Collard Greens

Ingredients

  • Collard greens
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  • Wash and chop collard greens.
  • Cook onion and garlic in a saucepan until soft.
  • Add greens and vegetable stock.
  • Simmer until greens are tender.
  • Season and serve warm.

Spinach and Feta Quiche

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Feta cheese
  • Pastry crust

Method

  • Preheat oven to 180°C.
  • Cook or boil spinach lightly and drain.
  • Beat eggs with milk and mix in feta cheese.
  • Combine spinach with egg mixture.
  • Pour into pastry crust and bake until firm and golden.

Kale Salad

Ingredients

  • Fresh kale
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Method

  • Wash kale leaves thoroughly and chop
  • Massage the leaves with olive oil until slightly softened.
  • Add lemon juice, garlic, and salt
  • Toss well and serve fresh
  • Add lemon, tomato, cheese for garnish.

Coleslaw

Ingredients

  • Shredded cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar

Method

  • Wash and finely shred the cabbage.
  • Grate the carrot and place it in a large mixing bowl with the cabbage.
  • In a separate bowl, mix the mayonnaise, lemon juice (or vinegar), sugar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  • Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrot mixture.
  • Toss well until all ingredients are evenly coated.
  • Chill in the refrigerator for about 10 minutes before serving for better flavour

Citrus Fruit Recipes

Tangerine Almond Cake

  • Tangerines
  • Eggs
  • Ground almonds
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder

Method

  • Boil the whole tangerines in water for 1.5 hours until soft. Drain and cool.
  • Pureeing: Pulse the whole fruits in a food processor until smooth
  • Beat eggs and sugar until thick.
  • Fold in the tangerine puree, ground almonds, and baking powder.
  • Pour into a lined tin and bake at 160°C for 50–60 mins until a skewer comes out clean.
  • The citrus pulp provides the entire structural moisture for this cake.

Zesty Lemon & Herb Roasted Chicken

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (approx. 1.5 kg)
  • 3 large organic lemons (2 juiced, 1 sliced)
  • 4 crushed garlic cloves
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

Method

  • Preheat oven to 200°C and place chicken in a heavy-bottomed pan.
  • Whisk lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and chopped herbs in a small bowl.
  • Rub mixture over chicken skin and inside cavity. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Place lemon slices and remaining herbs inside cavity.
  • Roast for 75 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 75°C and skin is golden.
  • Rest for 10 minutes before carving.

Orange Glazed Salmon

  • Salmon
  • Orange juice
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger

Method

  • Combine orange juice, honey, soy sauce, ginger, and garlic in a saucepan.
  • Cook over medium heat until slightly thick.
  • Place salmon fillets in a baking dish and season lightly.
  • Pour the orange glaze over the salmon.
  • Bake at 180°C for 15–20 minutes until cooked.

Key Lime Cheesecake

  • Digestive biscuits
  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Lime juice
  • Whipped cream

Method

  • Crush biscuits and mix with melted butter to form the crust.
  • Press into a cake pan and chill.
  • Mix cream cheese, sugar, lime juice, and zest until smooth.
  • Fold in whipped cream.
  • Pour filling onto the crust and refrigerate at least 4 hours.

Lemon Marmalade

Ingredients

  • 450g lemons
  • 450g sugar
  • 7½ cups water

Method

  • Wash and dry lemons. Peel the rind thinly into narrow strips.
  • Chop the rind into thin strips and set aside.
  • Cut lemons in half and squeeze out the juice, saving the pips.
  • Remove yellow pith, leaving the pulp.
  • Chop the pith roughly, tie it with the pips in muslin.
  • Cut pulp into small chunks and put in a pan with the peel and muslin bag.
  • Strain lemon juice into the pan and add water.
  • Boil over low heat, simmer uncovered for 1–1½ hours until peel is soft and liquid reduces by half.
  • Remove muslin bag, add sugar, and stir until dissolved.

Conclusion

Citrus fruits and leafy greens serve as vital dietary staples, packed with vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants that enhance immune function, promote development, cardiovascular wellness, and digestive health. Greens including spinach, kale, and chard supply iron, folate, and vitamin K, whereas citrus options like oranges, lemons, and limes offer vitamin C and potassium. Five recipes for each highlight their adaptability in budget-friendly, appealing meals, such as salads enduring healthy eating practices and kitchen innovation for thriving families.
